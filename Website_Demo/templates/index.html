Welcome

This project centers around the integration of machine learning and visual analytics to explore the emotional connection between New York Times articles and their corresponding reader comments.

This project aims to analyze and visualize the emotional aspects of comments, providing valuable insights to media outlets, enabling them to better understand reader preferences and improve content quality and business value.

By analyzing comment sentiment, outlets can better understand reader preferences for different themes and styles of content, allowing for more informed decision-making in editorial planning and content strategy.
