Conclusion




The Analysis Conclusion of the project:

Sentiment shift

Sentiment shift from political to social: in 2017, the focus was on political controversies (e.g., “Trump” and “Russiagate”), while in 2018, it shifted to social issues (e.g., gun violence), with a significant increase in negative sentiment.

Keyword network

Keyword network centrality: the core keyword “people” highlights the public's concern about social justice and policy implications, and is strongly associated with keywords such as “government” and “law”.

Writing trends

Sentiment and keyword-driven writing trends: Positive sentiment reflects public expectations, negative sentiment focuses on controversial events, and mainstream neutral sentiment supports broader, objective discussion.
